Our Story

Rooted in passion, bloomed with purpose — Trinidad's favourite florist since 2018.

Who We Are

Bloom & Thorn Florist is a Trinidad-based florist company operating a "click-and-mortar" retail model. We serve retail consumers (B2C) and corporate clients (B2B) through three physical locations and our e-commerce website at www.bloomandthorn.com.

Founded by passionate florists who believed that every arrangement should tell a story, we have grown from a single Port of Spain boutique to a team of over 20 dedicated floral artists and delivery specialists across the island.

Browse Our Collections

🌿 Our Mission

To create meaningful connections through the art of flowers — delivering joy, comfort, and beauty to every corner of Trinidad, one arrangement at a time.

👁 Our Vision

To be the Caribbean's most trusted and innovative floral brand, blending technology with craftsmanship to redefine the flower gifting experience.

💚 Our Values

Craftsmanship, sustainability, accessibility, and community. We source locally where possible and donate unsold arrangements to hospitals and community centres weekly.

Our Journey

From a small corner shop to a beloved island-wide brand — here are the milestones that shaped who we are today.

2018

The First Bloom

Amara opens the first Bloom & Thorn location on Frederick Street, Port of Spain, with just four team members.

2020

Expanding South

The San Fernando branch opens, serving the south Trinidad community with same-day delivery across the region.

2022

Corporate Partnerships

Major hotel chains and event companies begin subscribing to our weekly floral service, launching our B2B division.

2023

Chaguanas Branch

Our third location opens at Mid Centre Mall, Chaguanas, completing our island-wide reach.

2025

Digital Launch

www.bloomandthorn.com goes live — bringing same-day delivery and full e-commerce capabilities to all of Trinidad.
