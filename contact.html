Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 12:00pm Monday–Saturday are eligible for same-day delivery across Trinidad.

Can I request a custom arrangement?

Absolutely. Use our contact form and select "Custom Arrangement" — our florists will reach out within 24 hours.

Do you cater for weddings and events?

Yes! We offer full wedding and event floristry packages. Contact our team for a free consultation and quote.
